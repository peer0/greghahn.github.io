About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science, specializing in formal language theory and computational linguistics. I am set to graduate soon and am currently researching M-unambiguous language and its relations with sparsity and probability convergence.

Research

My research interests include:

  • M-equivalence and language sparsity
  • Formal language theory
  • Computational complexity
  • Parikh matrix mapping
